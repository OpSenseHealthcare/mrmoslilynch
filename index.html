ABOUT BLADDER PROSTATE MEN'S HEALTH FEES Mr Conor Mösli Lynch FRCS CONSULTANT UROLOGICAL SURGEON 25 HARLEY STREET, LONDON Conor Mösli Lynch is a Consultant Urological Surgeon. He specialises primarily in the diagnosis and treatment of bladder issues including urinary incontinence, outflow obstruction and recurrent infections. He graduated from Imperial College School of Medicine with First Class Honours, Distinction in Medical Finals and was awarded the 2010 Distinction in Clinical Practice. He trained at the three specialist centres in Central London (UCL, Imperial College & St Bartholomew's Hospitals) with a sub-speciality interest in pelvic and reconstructive surgery. He was elected a Fellow of The Royal College of Surgeons in 2021 and became a Consultant Surgeon at The Chelsea & Westminster at the age of 34. In his spare time he collects classic cars (Bristols) and rare-breed sheep (Jacobs). He is married and divides his time between London, Herefordshire and Scotland.
