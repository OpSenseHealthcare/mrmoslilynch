Mr Conor Mösli Lynch FRCS

Consultant Urological Surgeon
25 Harley Street, London

Conor Mösli Lynch is a Consultant Urological Surgeon. He specialises primarily in the diagnosis and treatment of bladder issues including urinary incontinence, outflow obstruction and recurrent infections.

He graduated from Imperial College School of Medicine with First Class Honours, Distinction in Medical Finals and was awarded the 2010 Distinction in Clinical Practice.

He trained at the three specialist centres in Central London (UCL, Imperial College & St Bartholomew's Hospitals) with a sub-speciality interest in pelvic and reconstructive surgery.

He was elected a Fellow of The Royal College of Surgeons in 2021 and became a Consultant Surgeon at The Chelsea & Westminster at the age of 34.

In his spare time he collects classic cars (Bristols) and rare-breed sheep (Jacobs). He is married and divides his time between London, Herefordshire and Scotland.

Prostate

  • Surgery for Enlarged Prostate
  • Raised PSA
  • Prostatitis

Mr Mösli Lynch is experienced in the diagnosis and treatment of prostatism, the condition caused by the natural enlargement of the prostate as we age. He is able to perform minimally invasive procedures to shrink the prostate and improve the symptoms related to poor urinary flow and incomplete bladder emptying.

As an ex-professional ballet dancer (Birmingham Royal Ballet) he is happy to offer pro-bono treatment to current dancers of listed corps-de-ballet via St Vitus's Foundation.
